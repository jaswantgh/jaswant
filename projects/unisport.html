The UniSport Nationals is a multi-sport event held annually between 43 Australian universities and tertiary institutions. In 2022 it was held in Perth (Whadjuk), Western Australia with over 5,000 participants competing across 26 sports from across the country. During my time as a volunteer with the Marketing and Communications team at UniSport Australia, I: * Assisted in preparing and delivering promotional and marketing activities in an international event environment by exploring different venues and capturing relevant content. * Assisted in the monitoring of social media engagement across all platforms and efficient and effective day-to-day communications with participants by re-sharing content and commenting and replying to the audience. * Assisted in Evaluating the value of the data and analytics and insights to communicate business outcomes to Stakeholders to maximize strategy performance. * Met with the CEO and Marketing and Communications Manager and assisted in managing content and coordinating social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, BeReal, and Twitter. * Improved my computing and mobile app skills by assisting in Branding & Designing social media posts, reels, and stories. * Assisted in content creation using the graphic design Canva App and the creation of gifs (via Giphy). * Updated Linktree and learned how to use SMS broadcast. * Assisted in timekeeping for Division 1 Nationals Swimming. * Contributions to the accreditation process of Division 1 Water Polo .
