What is Kiwanis?

Kiwanis International is a worldwide organization with over 550,000 members in 80 countries, focused on enhancing children's lives via localized projects tackling issues like hunger and literacy. Their local clubs, driven by member initiatives, carry out almost 150,000 service projects annually.

My Role

As a club secretary for Kiwanis International, I am presently serving in Division 8, the Perth District. My primary responsibilities include meticulous minute-taking during our monthly meetings and subsequently crafting a comprehensive report encapsulating the key points and decisions from these meetings. Additionally, I am tasked with the preparation of a new agenda for each upcoming meeting, ensuring that our focus remains centered on our goals and key action items. My role is crucial in maintaining the smooth functioning and effective communication within our team as we continue to strive for the betterment of children's lives in our community.
